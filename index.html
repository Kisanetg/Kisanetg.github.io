What is Temple-Run?

Temple Run is a 3D endless running video game. The game controls an explorer who obtained an ancient relic and is running from evil demon monkeys who are chasing him. Along the way the explore can obtain coins and prizes that will help him along his journey. This particular game is for all ages and is also fairly easy to learn.
