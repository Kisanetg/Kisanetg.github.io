Programs and More:

Available to all residents & low income Families.

Yesler Terrace has many different programs that cater to all families. These opportunities are created to support families who are in need. Below are multiple organizations that are open to families that live under Seattle Housing.

Alefesh Tiku Childcare

Yesler Terrace ECEAP is a child care center facility which provides day care services for all children. Children who experience quality early education and child care in YESLER TERRACE ECEAP in the city of Seattle receive long term benefits. They have better math, language and social skills as they enter school, and, as they grow older in the county of King , state of Washington, they will require less special education and will progress further in school. This day care is located in the Seattle in the county of King, in the state of WA. Several research studies have found that high quality child/day care centers have common characteristics, especially near Seattle.

Address: 115 8th Ave #180, Seattle, WA 98104

Contact Information: (206) 228-4507

Yesler Youth Tutoring Program (Catholic Services)

YTP is an after-school educational enrichment program for first through twelfth-grade students who live in six low-income and public housing communities in Seattle. Started as a partnership with the Seattle Housing Authority in 1991, the tutoring centers provide youth with a safe, positive, and stimulating environment to explore learning and experience academic and personal success. Volunteers are the heart of the program. They work with students individually or in pairs to follow each student’s individualized learning plan. At the tutoring centers, students build skills in reading and math, get help with homework, and grow their confidence. There are six YTP tutoring centers located throughout Seattle. Centers are open in the late afternoon and early evening hours. YTP students are wonderfully diverse, representing many nationalities, cultures, languages, and religions.


Address: 120 8th Ave, Seattle, WA 98104

Contact Information: ((206) 682-5590

NeighborCare Health

NeighborCare health operates a Community Health Worker (CHW) program in Yesler Terrace partnership with SHA and other grant partners. They hired 4 Community Health Workers in Yesler Terrace to better connect people with primary care and health-related activities in the community. The program goals are to: increase community access to appropriate healthcare resources; improve individual health outcomes by increasing the percent of residents who describe their health as being “good” or “excellent”; decrease the percent of residents using the Emergency Room as their primary source of care; and improve community mental health of those who are depressed or hopeless nearly every day.


Address: : 905 Spruce St Seattle, WA 98104

Contact Information: (206) 548-3069

Job Link

Seattle Housing Authority’s Job Link connects tenants to employment, education and resources. The program can help tenants find a job right away, enroll in training and education and then find a job; get a better job; or start a small business.
