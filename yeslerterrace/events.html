Yesler Outdoor Summer Movie Nights:

Where: : Yesler Terrace Park!

Everyone is invited to the 2019 Yesler Outdoor Summer Movie Nights! Come early and enjoy the beautiful views and sunset! The screen will be set up on the west lawn, near the intersection of Yesler Way and South Washington Street. Bring blankets and enjoy the show!

  • 7/19/2019: Ferris Bueller's Day Off
  • 7/26/2019: Ghostbusters
  • 8/2/2019: The Goonies
  • 8/9/2019: Hidden Figures
  • 8/16/2019: Black Panther
