More about our community:

Yesler Terrace is a 22-acre public housing development located in Seattle, Washington. At the time of its completion in 1941, Washington State's first public housing was the first racially integrated public housing development in the United States. Yesler Terrace is currently under redevelopment to build a stronger and safer area for residents. Many families that live in the neighborhood are from all walks of life and contribute to it by having gatherings to socialize, and other important meetings.
